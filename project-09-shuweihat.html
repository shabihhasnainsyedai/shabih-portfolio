Overview

The Shuweihat Water Transmission Scheme was a landmark infrastructure program for the Emirate of Abu Dhabi — a high-value, technically complex water transmission network serving the region's growing population and industrial base. At $850M, it was one of the most significant capital programs in Abu Dhabi's early-2000s expansion.

PROJACS International was engaged as Project Management Consultant. The mandate: implement Primavera Expedition across a team of 60 professionals working from 4 geographically distributed sites. What was planned to take 2 years was delivered in 6 months. This project was the beginning of a 23-year career.

Challenge & Solution
⚠ Challenge

4 sites, 2-year plan, no cloud tools

Implementing Primavera Expedition across 4 distributed UAE sites before cloud deployment existed — all technical infrastructure, user access, configuration, and training had to be delivered in a fully coordinated manner with 2 years allocated in the plan.

✦ Solution

Citrix deployment — live in 6 months

Architected a Citrix-based remote access solution enabling consistent, centrally managed platform access across all 4 sites. Delivered a fully operational Primavera Expedition system in 6 months through disciplined parallel-track delivery.

6 Months

Go-live — against a 2-year planned timeline

A 75% compression of the planned implementation timeline, achieved through disciplined delivery and Citrix-based multi-site architecture. The first of many programs delivered ahead of every expectation across a 23-year career.

Solution Delivery
1
