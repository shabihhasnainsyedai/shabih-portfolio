Overview

Trans Alta is one of Canada's largest publicly traded power generators, operating wind, solar, hydro, and natural gas facilities across North America. Their C-suite depended on BI reporting to make critical operational and strategic decisions — but the BI infrastructure had become a serious bottleneck.

Critical reports were taking 40–50 minutes to execute. By the time data was available, decisions had already been made on outdated information. The root cause: unoptimised Denodo virtual data models, inefficient VQL queries, and integration patterns creating unnecessary computational overhead across multiple source systems.

Challenge & Opportunity
⚠ Challenge

Denodo virtual data models had been built iteratively without performance optimisation as a priority. Queries pulled from multiple sources — databases, APIs, cloud platforms, flat files — through inefficient joins, missing caching, and unbounded scopes that forced full data scans on every execution. C-suite confidence in BI as a decision tool was eroding.

✦ Opportunity

A systematic rebuild of Denodo VQL models — with proper caching, push-down optimisation, restructured joins, and role-based security — could deliver near-real-time performance without any new infrastructure investment. The entire gain was achievable through engineering discipline alone.

Solution Delivery
1

Query Performance Diagnostic

Conducted a full audit of existing Denodo base views, derived views, and join strategies to identify the specific patterns causing 40–50 minute execution times. Profiled each bottleneck individually before touching any code.

2

Denodo VQL Model Reconstruction

Rebuilt virtual data models from the ground up — implementing push-down query optimisation, eliminating redundant derived views, and restructuring joins to minimise data movement across the virtualisation layer.

3

Caching Configuration & Refresh Strategy

Implemented intelligent caching at the Denodo layer for frequently accessed datasets — configuring full and partial cache modes with scheduled refresh cycles that balanced freshness against performance.

4

Role-Based Security & Data Masking

Designed and implemented role-based security policies and data masking within Denodo — ensuring sensitive operational and financial data was accessible only to authorised users, with appropriate masking for lower-clearance roles.

5

5 Executive Power BI Dashboards

Built 5 executive-level Power BI dashboards connected to the rebuilt Denodo layer — transforming complex multi-source energy datasets into clear narratives for 8–10 C-suite stakeholders.

6

COVID Capacity Compliance Dashboard

When COVID-19 required office capacity management, delivered a real-time Power BI monitoring dashboard in 2 months — enabling HR to track employee attendance and enforce safe occupancy limits across all Trans Alta facilities.
