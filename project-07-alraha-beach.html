Overview

The Al Raha Beach Development was a landmark $20B+ master-planned waterfront community on Abu Dhabi's coast — one of the most ambitious real estate developments in Gulf history. Developed by ALDAR Laing O'Rourke, the program encompassed residential towers, commercial districts, hotels, marinas, and extensive infrastructure across multiple phases spanning many years.

Managing contracts, documentation, variations, and reporting at this scale required enterprise-grade systems deployed consistently across all contractors, consultants, and project teams. This became one of the largest Primavera Contract Manager deployments ever executed in the Middle East — 1,000 active users operating simultaneously across the full program.

Challenge & Opportunity
⚠ Challenge

Rolling out PCM to 1,000 users across a multi-billion dollar program meant training hundreds of professionals from diverse backgrounds — contractors, engineers, document controllers, commercial managers — on a complex platform, in a compressed timeframe, while live project operations continued without interruption.

✦ Opportunity

A structured role-based training approach — combined with robust platform configuration aligned to ALDAR Laing O'Rourke's specific workflows — could drive adoption at scale and establish PCM as the single source of truth for all contract and document management across the program.

Solution Delivery
1

PCM Platform Configuration

Configured Primavera Contract Manager to align with ALDAR Laing O'Rourke's specific contract structures, document control workflows, approval hierarchies, and reporting requirements — ensuring the platform reflected actual business processes.

2

Role-Based Training Program for 500–600 Users

Designed and delivered a comprehensive role-based training program covering 500–600 users over 12 months — tailored separately for document controllers, commercial managers, engineers, and project directors.

3

ACONEX Document Management Integration

Managed the interface between Primavera Contract Manager and ACONEX — ensuring document registers, transmittals, and correspondence tracked in ACONEX were properly referenced and linked within PCM.

4

Monthly Executive Dashboard Delivery

Built and delivered monthly executive dashboards aggregating data from ACONEX, Primavera CM, and Primavera P6 — consolidating document status, contract commitments, cost performance, and schedule progress for senior leadership.

5

Application Stewardship & Platform Governance

Served as Application Steward for PCM across the entire program — managing system administration, user access, data quality, workflow configurations, and platform governance throughout the deployment lifecycle.
