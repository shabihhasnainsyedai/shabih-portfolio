Overview

Cenovus Energy is one of Canada's largest oil and gas companies, operating major oil sands projects in northern Alberta. Across their construction and capital projects portfolio, contracts management was a critical — and critically inefficient — function.

Cost Engineers responsible for tracking contract spend, purchase orders, receipts, and invoices were spending 40–50 hours per week manually extracting data from legacy Microsoft Access and Excel systems, then manually reconciling it against Maximo for monthly reporting. The Supply Chain Management Transformation (SCMT) program was the initiative to fix this.

Challenge & Opportunity
⚠ Challenge

Cost Engineers were spending 40–50 hours per week manually extracting cost data from Access and Excel systems, then reconciling it against Maximo for monthly reporting. The reconciliation alone — bridging Maximo's asset management data with Oracle's contract records — was taking several full weeks every month, with frequent discrepancies.

✦ Opportunity

Oracle Contract Management offered a purpose-built platform for automating cost reporting, contract tracking, and PO management. The key unlock was an 8-feed integration with Maximo that could automate the entire monthly reconciliation — replacing weeks of manual work with a 2–3 hour process.

Solution Delivery
1

Requirements Discovery & Contract Lifecycle Mapping

Conducted detailed analysis with Cost Engineers, Finance leads, and Supply Chain managers to map the complete contract lifecycle — from AFE approval through purchase orders, receipts, vouchers, and invoices — identifying every data point needed between Oracle CM and Maximo.

2

Oracle Contract Management Implementation

Designed and implemented Oracle Contract Management across all Cenovus oil sands projects — configuring the system to automate cost reporting, replacing the manual Access/Excel extraction process with a system-driven workflow.

3

8-Feed Maximo Integration Architecture

Architected and built an 8-feed bidirectional data integration between Maximo and Oracle CM — mapping hundreds of fields across both systems covering AFEs, Contracts, Purchase Orders, Standard Vouchers, Receipts, Invoices, Cost Actuals, and Commitments.

4

Oracle BI Reporting & Publisher Configuration

Designed comprehensive Oracle BI reports and Publisher templates consolidating AFEs, contracts, POs, vouchers, receipts, and invoices into a unified reporting system — complete contract cost visibility with zero manual aggregation.

5

Agile Delivery Across the SCMT Program

Managed project schedules and delivery using Agile/Scrum methodology — ensuring sprint-based delivery kept the program on track and aligned to evolving business priorities.
