Transforming Enterprises Through Technology & Intelligence

Shabih ul Hasnain is a seasoned AI Transformation and Digital Strategy Leader with over two decades of proven impact across construction, energy, aviation, and public infrastructure.

Currently serving as Sr. Manager of Digital Transformation at El-Seif Engineering & Contracting in Saudi Arabia, Shabih leads enterprise-wide AI platform adoption, intelligent automation, and data modernization initiatives — delivering measurable outcomes including the recovery of hundreds of hours per week through automation and real-time C-suite visibility.

He has deployed enterprise platforms to thousands of users, migrated terabytes of critical data, and built governance frameworks that align entire organizations around a single source of truth.

Shabih bridges the gap between business complexity and technology possibility — translating organizational challenges into scalable AI and digital solutions that create sustainable competitive advantage.
