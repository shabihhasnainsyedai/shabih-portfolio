Overview

Construction organizations have historically lagged in AI adoption — not for lack of opportunity, but because of the complexity of their operations and the gap between available AI tools and practical deployment knowledge. At El-Seif Engineering & Contracting, this gap represented a significant opportunity.

The mandate was to identify high-impact manual workflows across all departments, design AI-powered alternatives, and deploy them at scale — starting with the workflows consuming the most time and producing the least value. The result: hundreds of hours per month recovered, AI embedded across all departments, and a 35% increase in self-service data tool adoption.

Challenge & Solution
⚠ Challenge

Hundreds of hours lost to repetitive tasks

Teams across project delivery and corporate functions spending hundreds of hours monthly on report compilation, data re-entry, document formatting, and ad hoc requests — consuming skilled professionals and slowing decisions.

✦ Solution

Claude, Copilot, n8n, Emergent, and Lovable deployed across departments — automating entire categories of work and delivering compounding time and cost savings.

Solution Delivery
1

High-Impact Use Case Discovery

Structured interviews and process mapping with department heads to identify workflows with the highest time cost and lowest strategic value — the prime targets for AI automation.

2

Executive Dashboard Automation

Built automated reporting pipelines using Power BI, SQL, and Claude — replacing 5–8 hours per week of manual leadership report compilation with live C-suite dashboards.

3

n8n Intelligent Workflow Automation

Deployed n8n connecting enterprise systems, triggering actions on data events, and routing information between platforms without manual intervention — always-on automation running 24/7.

4

Claude & Copilot Across Teams

Implemented Claude and Copilot to accelerate document drafting, report writing, email composition, and knowledge retrieval — reducing time-to-output for knowledge work by 40–60%.

5

AI Literacy & Adoption Program

Designed and delivered a company-wide AI enablement program with hands-on training — increasing self-service tool adoption by 35% across all departments.
