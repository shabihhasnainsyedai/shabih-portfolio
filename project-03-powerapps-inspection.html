Overview

Site inspections are a fundamental daily activity on any major construction project — recording defects, verifying workmanship, documenting progress, and flagging safety issues. At El-Seif, this process was entirely manual: paper SOR forms, hand-written inspection reports, physical photo printing, and laborious data re-entry back at the office.

The gap between what happened on site and what was visible to project managers was creating delays, rework, and blind spots in quality management. The solution had to be mobile-first, easy for site engineers to adopt, and capable of feeding directly into a centralised database without any manual transcription.

Challenge & Opportunity
⚠ Challenge

Site engineers were spending 4–5 hours per week completing paper forms, manually printing and attaching photos, then physically filing or scanning documents. Data was often incomplete, delayed by days, and impossible to aggregate or analyse across projects.

✦ Opportunity

Microsoft PowerApps offered the capability to build a custom mobile inspection app deployable on company iPads — with native camera integration, SharePoint connectivity for real-time storage, and form logic that enforces data completeness at the point of entry.

Solution Delivery
1

Process Discovery & Form Mapping

Worked with site engineers and QA managers to map every field, photo type, and approval step in existing paper forms — ensuring the digital solution captured everything required without adding complexity.

2

PowerApps Application Build

Designed and built a custom PowerApps solution with intuitive inspection forms, native device camera integration for real-time photo capture, and validation rules ensuring data completeness before submission.

3

SharePoint Integration & Central Database

Connected PowerApps to SharePoint as the central data repository — all inspection records, photos, and form data stored, indexed, and immediately queryable from any device.

4

Power Automate Notification Workflows

Configured Power Automate flows to automatically notify stakeholders when inspections are submitted, defects are flagged, or approvals are required — replacing informal verbal notification chains.

5

iPad Deployment & On-Site Training

Deployed the solution on company iPads across all site teams. Delivered hands-on on-site training sessions ensuring engineers were fully confident before paper processes were retired.
